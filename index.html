About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Julia Huynh. I'm a student at Wilfrid Laurier, studying User Experience Design and minoring in psychology. I come from an art background and I love designing and coming up with new ideas. I enjoy designing and actively participate in design events at my school.
